About Our Ethiopian Coffee Export

Licensed Exporter of Premium Ethiopian Coffee

Datan Utukana Malo is a licensed and registered exporter of premium Ethiopian coffee and coffee leaves from the Oromia region. With deep roots in Ethiopia's rich coffee culture, we bring the world's finest coffee beans from the highlands of Guji, Ethiopia to global markets.

Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and fair trade practices ensures that every cup of our coffee delivers an authentic Ethiopian experience while supporting local Oromo farming communities.

We offer both dried and undried coffee varieties, preserving traditional processing methods that enhance the unique flavors and aromas of genuine Ethiopian coffee.

Our Ethiopian Coffee Products

Dried Coffee Beans

Sun-dried to perfection, our dried coffee beans from Ethiopia's Guji region offer a rich, full-bodied flavor with low acidity and notes of chocolate and spice.

Undried Coffee Beans

Our undried coffee beans preserve their natural moisture, resulting in a brighter, more complex flavor profile with fruity and floral notes characteristic of Ethiopian coffee.

Coffee Leaves

Harvested from the same plants as our premium beans in Oromia, our coffee leaves are perfect for traditional Ethiopian coffee leaf tea, a healthy alternative beverage.
